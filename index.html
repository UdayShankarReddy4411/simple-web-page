A Page About My Hero...

He is a super star.
But here in the image he appears to be a gangster.

Uday was not the kind of hero you see in movies. He did not have magical powers or wear a special costume. His strength was of a different kind. He was known throughout his village for his calm mind and helpful nature. If two families had an argument, Vikram was the one who could make them listen to each other and find a peaceful solution. He was also very observant. He could notice a small crack in a dam before it became a big problem, or see that a family was struggling even when they didn't ask for help. His greatest ability was his knack for bringing people together. When the village needed a new well, it was Vikram who organised everyone, making the hard work feel like a community festival. He never wanted praise; for him, the happiness and safety of his people was the only reward.
